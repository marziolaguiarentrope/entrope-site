Frequently Asked Questions

                

                

                    

                        
                            When should I choose a Fixed-Scope Project vs. a Dedicated Team?
                            
                        
                        
A **Fixed-Scope Project** is perfect for solving a specific problem with a defined scope. The **Dedicated Team** works like a 'squad as a service,' ideal for companies with an automation roadmap that need a continuous partner to evolve their processes.

                    

                    

                        
                            Why do you use n8n?
                             
                        
                        
n8n is an open-source automation platform that gives us total flexibility to build complex workflows, connect to any API, and ensure the solution is scalable and secure, running on your own infrastructure if needed.

                    

                    

                        
                            How are projects priced?
                             
                        
                        
For **Fixed-Scope Projects**, the price is fixed based on the scope. For a **Dedicated Team**, we work with a monthly retainer based on hour allocation. All proposals are detailed and have no surprises.
